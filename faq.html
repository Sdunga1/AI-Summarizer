Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get a Gemini API key?

Visit Google AI Studio and follow the steps to generate your free API key.

Why does the extension ask to reload the page?

The extension needs to hook into the article’s DOM. Reloading ensures proper initialization.

Is my data stored or tracked?

No. The extension stores your Gemini API key only on your local Chrome storage. We do not store, share, or transmit any user data.

How do I analyze code with this extension?

Paste your code in the textarea at the bottom of the popup. Choose “Big-Oh” or “Detailed” and click “Analyze Code.”
